Arm exercises are an essential part of any workout routine. They help to build and tone the muscles in your arms, which can improve your overall strength and appearance. There are many different exercises that you can do to work your arms, including:

1 / 3
This exercise targets the biceps, which are the muscles on the front of your upper arm. Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing up. Keep your elbows close to your body and curl the weights up towards your shoulders. Lower the weights back down to the starting position and repeat.
2 / 3
This exercise targets the triceps, which are the muscles on the back of your upper arm. Sit on the edge of a chair or bench with your hands placed next to your hips. Slide your butt off the chair and lower yourself down towards the floor, bending your elbows. Push yourself back up to the starting position and repeat.
3 / 3
This exercise targets the shoulders. Hold a dumbbell in each hand at shoulder height with your palms facing forward. Press the weights up towards the ceiling, keeping your elbows close to your body. Lower the weights back down to the starting position and repeat.
